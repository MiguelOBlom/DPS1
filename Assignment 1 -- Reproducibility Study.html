Document structure and consistency

Well-structured: each section has a clear and unique function. Hierarchy of sections is correct. Ordering of sections is logical. All information occurs at the correct place. Level of detail is appropriate throughout. The material is overall balanced.

Most sections have a clear and unique function. Hierarchy of sections is mostly correct. Ordering of sections is mostly logical. Information occurs at the correct place, with few exceptions.  In most places the level of detail is appropriate.

Main structure correct, but placement of material in different chapters illogical in places. Level of detail inappropriate in a number of places (irrelevant information given). The material is sometimes imbalanced.

Main structure is correct, but lower level hierarchy of sections is not logical in places. Some sections have overlapping functions leading to ambiguity in placement of information. Level of detail varies widely.

Clarity and readability

Formulations in text are clear and exact, and concise in relationship with the intended audience.The grammar and punctuation are of high quality, and the vocabulary is typical for the field.

Formulations in text are predominantly clear and exact, and concise for some audience. The grammar and punctuation are of good quality, and the vocabulary matches the field.

The text is mostly reader-friendly. The thesis should have been written more concisely. The grammar and/or punctuation are lacking, and the vocabulary matches only sometimes the field

The text is ambiguous in some places but this does not always inhibit correct interpretation. The grammar and/or punctuation could be significantly improved , and the vocabulary matches only sometimes the field

Use of graphs, tables, diagrams

Information is supported by appropriate visuals, tables, listings, etc.

Information is generally supported by appropriate visuals, tables, listings, etc.

Information is often supported by appropriate visuals, tables, listings, etc.

Information is sometimes not supported by appropriate visuals, tables, listings, etc.
